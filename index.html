DETAILS ON SCHOOL:

The Navabharath higher secondary school, Attingal-is run and managed by the Navabharath Vignan Trust (Reg.No.Q.904/79).The school was inaguurated by late muhammed Koya, the hon'ble minister of education,Government of Kerala on 12th june 1979.English is the medium of instuction. The school designs to ensure the development of multiple intelligence,imaginative faculities,aesthetic skills'individual talents and critical thinking among the children.The school aims at providing quality eduacation at an affordable cost so that it does not become the privilege of the affluent alone.

SCOUTS AND GUIDES:

Two troops of the Scout wing(Boys) nd two companies of Guide(Girls) are functiong in the school.Basically, pupils of 6 standard get membership every year and such Scout cadets and Guide cadets have to undergo the activities, trainig and other courses as prescribed during the studies from standard 6 to 10.They have to attend the camps and pass the examination (Pravesh, Prathama sopan,Dwitiya sopan,Tritiy sopan,Rajya puraskar,Rashtrapathi award).
