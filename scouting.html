THE HISTORY OF SCOUTING AND
GUIDING IN THE WORLD

*The boy scout movement has its simple start in the year 1907 when a
retired army general lord Baden Powell conducted an experimental camp
in Brown sea island in England with 20 boys.The successful canmp and
publication of the book "Scouting For Boys" in a fortnightly marked the
start of the scout movement.
*In the year 1910, Crystal palace ralley was held where the girls wearing
the boy scout uniform appeared and wanted to join the scout movement.Lord
Baden Powell decided to start a movement of girls with the help of his
sister Agnes Baden Powell.
