THE BHARATH SCOUT AND GUIDE

SCOUT GUIDE SALUTE

Scout/Guide salute is given by the raising the right arms smartly to the shoulder,palm to the front with the 3 fingers sretched together, the first touching the forehead one inch above the right eyebrow and thumb closing on the little finger.The 3 fingers held upright represent the 3 fold promise.
